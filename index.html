What is it And Why care?

Sustainable Farming is a way to farm in a environmentally responsible way that keeps our soils healthy. the the way we farm today as a huge impact on more than just crops, industrial farming leads to soil degradation, can pollute water, and leave food with fewer nutrients. if we dont start caring about the soil, we will struggle in the future to find fertile land to grow food. Sustainable farming gives us a opportunity to help preserve our planet, grow more nutrient dense food, and to make sure farmers have jobs for generations.
