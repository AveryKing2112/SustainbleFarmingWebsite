Sustainable Farming

I started my journey to sustainable low cost farming only a few years ago. It can really seem like alot of hocus pocus and nonsense with these ideas and it can be hard to believe its effectiveness. That is why I wanted to make this website, to show how its actually extremely easy to do and understand as long as you are willing to learn, and put a little effort. My goal is to hopefully increase the amount of sustainable agriculture that is happening in hopes to preserve our planet.
